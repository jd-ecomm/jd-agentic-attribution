Summary
What
AI and LLM platforms generated $51K last-click and $63.5K first-click net revenue since Jan 2025, from 32K sessions and 469 orders. ChatGPT dominates at 96% of attributed revenue. The channel had zero revenue attribution in Jan 2025 and reached a peak of $9.7K in December 2025. Weekly share of total revenue has grown from near-zero to 0.2–0.4% in recent weeks, with first-click share reaching 0.44% in late April 2026.
So What
This is a structurally emerging channel, not a statistical blip. The first-click premium over last-click (24% higher) confirms AI acts as a discovery and consideration touchpoint rather than a converter. Sessions grew from Q1 2025 with revenue conversion lagging by several months, consistent with a new channel building trust. The channel is still tiny relative to total revenue, but growth trajectory from Q3 2025 onward is consistent and accelerating.
What's Next
Monitor weekly share trajectory for continued growth above 0.3% last-click. Evaluate whether JD Sports Canada product content is being surfaced accurately in ChatGPT Shopping and Perplexity product answers. Perplexity and Copilot are small but present; consider whether structured data or feed optimisation could improve AI platform discoverability. Establish a monthly review cadence for AI channel share alongside paid and organic.
